01 Getting Started

Download SkinMap and create an account with your email or Google sign-in.

Grant camera access when prompted — this is required to perform skin scans.

Take your first scan from the home screen and receive your results within seconds.

02 Skin Scans

How accurate is my skin scan?

SkinMap analyzes your photo using advanced imaging technology to assess hydration, texture, tone, and other visible skin characteristics. Results are intended for informational and tracking purposes, not as a medical diagnosis.

Why was my scan rejected or unclear?

Make sure you're in even, natural lighting, facing the camera directly, with your face free of makeup and centered in the frame. Avoid heavy shadows or backlighting.

Can I see my scan history?

Yes — all past scans are saved to your profile so you can track changes in your skin over time.

03 Subscriptions

What's included in each plan?

Free includes limited scans per month. Basic and Premium unlock additional scans, deeper analysis, and consultation bookings with skincare professionals.

How do I cancel or change my plan?

Go to Profile → Subscription within the app to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.

Do you offer refunds?

Refund requests are handled case by case — contact us directly and we'll help sort it out.

04 Account & Privacy

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your scans, results, and personal data from our systems.

Is my scan data private?

Yes. Your scans and results are private to your account and are not shared with third parties without your consent. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
